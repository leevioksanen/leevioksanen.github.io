HealthyRecipes - Figma®


Here is an idea I developed for a mobile application to help follow a healthy diet and lifestyle. The application is called HealthyRecipes. In the project, I used an earthy and wellness-inspired color theme. The project highlights mastering basic skills in the Figma program. During the project, I learned to create components and different variations of scrolling. The idea of the application is to provide users with healthy yet nutritious meals and recipes. The app would allow users to create their own meal schedules and connect with professionals related to well-being. The amount of content is limited, as the project's focus was on exploring Figma's functionalities and the UI/UX design of the application. The video below demonstrates how the application would function in practice. All layouts and UI designs were developed by me, adhering to the universal design patterns of mobile applications.

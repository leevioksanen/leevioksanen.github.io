About Me

Creative artist

I am a third-year computing science student and member of the Games Academy at Tampere University of Applied Sciences. In the Games Academy I get to contribute in designing and creating games. My interests lie in 3D modeling, UI/UX design, and graphical design. As a graphic artist my job includes tasks such as the creation of visual elements and usability design.

I have a profound interest in visual projects and effects. However, I would say my top interests are 3D modeling, usability design, and UI/UX design. My eagerness to learn new skills and high motivation have helped me master the use of new platforms and become proficient in graphic design over a short period of time.

In the future I aspire to continue developing my skills as a graphic artist and aim to assist companies to express their brands through graphic design. Please take a look at my portfolio, where you can find projects that I have gotten to work with so far.

About Me

Current status

I am a Computer Science graduate and a former member of the Games Academy at Tampere University of Applied Sciences. During my studies, I focused on graphic design, with my strongest expertise in UI/UX design. I have experience designing user interfaces, visual elements, and user-centered experiences that balance usability with clear visual identity.

Although my background includes game development, my skills are highly adaptable across different industries. My main interests include UI/UX design, usability design, 3D modeling, and visual design. I am quick to learn new tools and platforms, highly motivated, and comfortable working in evolving environments.

I also have experience working with AI-related systems, giving me an understanding of how intelligent technologies and user-centered design work together. I am a calm, reliable person who is ready to take responsibility and contribute thoughtfully to projects. I am now looking for opportunities to apply my skills and grow as a designer.

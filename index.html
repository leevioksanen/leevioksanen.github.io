About Me

Current status

I am a Computer Science graduate and a former member of the Games Academy at Tampere University of Applied Sciences. During my time at the university, I contributed to the design and development of several games, focusing on 3D modeling, UI/UX design, and graphical design. As a graphic designer, I created visual elements and improved usability to ensure a seamless and engaging user experience.

My studies also included much more than just game development. I am highly interested in opportunities beyond the game industry as my skills are adaptable and applicable across various industries. I would say my top interests are usability design, UI/UX design and 3D modelling. During my studies, my eagerness to learn new skills and high motivation have helped me master the use of new platforms and become proficient in graphic design. I am ready to take the next steps in my career and apply my skills to new challenges and opportunities.

Looking ahead, I aspire to further develop my skills as a graphic designer and help companies express their brands through impactful design. Feel free to explore my portfolio to see the projects I have worked on so far.

About Me

Creative artist

I am a graduating Computer Science student and a member of the Games Academy at Tampere University of Applied Sciences. At the Games Academy, I have contributed to the design and development of games, focusing on 3D modeling, UI/UX design, and graphical design. As a graphic artist, my work involves creating visual elements and enhancing usability to ensure a seamless user experience.

I am also highly interested in opportunities beyond the game industry as my skills are adaptable and applicable across various industries. I would say my top interests are usability design and UI/UX design. During my studies, my eagerness to learn new skills and high motivation have helped me master the use of new platforms and become proficient in graphic design. I am ready to take the next steps in my career and apply my skills to new challenges and opportunities.

Looking ahead, I aspire to further develop my skills as a graphic artist and help companies express their brands through impactful design. Feel free to explore my portfolio to see the projects I have worked on so far.
